Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

TorrentX is a free Android application that provides high-performance BitTorrent downloading with a focus on privacy, speed, and simplicity.

We believe in transparency: TorrentX does not collect identifying personal information. We solely utilize anonymous crash reporting to maintain app stability and improve performance.

1. What TorrentX Does

TorrentX offers full BitTorrent support using a native engine, automatic 4K/HDR muxing, background execution, and advanced I/O optimization.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or personal identifiers
  • No advertising IDs or device fingerprinting for marketing
  • No cloud accounts or centralized login systems

3. Diagnostics & Crash Reporting

To identify bugs and resolve crashes in our native engine, TorrentX uses a third-party analytics service (Google Firebase Crashlytics). If the app crashes, the following anonymous data is collected strictly for analytics and debugging:

  • Crash Logs: Stack traces identifying the line of code that failed.
  • Diagnostics: Device state at the time of the crash (e.g., OS version, memory usage).
  • Device IDs: An anonymous, randomly generated UUID to measure the number of users experiencing a specific issue.

This data is transmitted securely, stored temporarily on Google's servers, and cannot be used to personally identify you.

4. Technical Information Processed Locally

The following information is handled only locally on your device:

  • Magnet links, .torrent files, and media URLs
  • Download progress, resume data, and settings

Note: Your IP address may be visible to other peers and trackers as part of standard BitTorrent protocol operation.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
By using TorrentX, you agree to this policy.
